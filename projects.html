FIT App

This is my favorite project as it was requested and is being used by the Syracuse Fire Department. FIT stands for Fire Incident Tracker and it used by the chief at the site of fire to manage teams, tasks, and time as the scene develops. A mobile cross platform for iPads and tablets, The FIT app is real world use application, densely packed with components that can track time, track tasks, and drag-and-drop items.This was a team project with one other student, and our first time using react and react native.

Great Salts

A mock company based out of Utah, Great Salts sells different types of... great salts from around the world including the Great Salt Lake in Utah. This website is connected to a database using PHP and MySQL. There is also user authentication that runs on sessions, allowing admins to edit the product and customer inventories straight form the website, and allows customers to purchase product from a paginated list of items. When a new customer registers, a verification email is sent to the user.

Uber Clone

This mobile app is a recreation of Uber, allowing the user to input a pickup and drop-off location. Directions, mapping, travel time, and travel distance are calculated and shown using Google Api's. Different prices options are shown for different types of vehicles. Redux is used to give global access throughout different pages and components.

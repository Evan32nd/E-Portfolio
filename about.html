About Me

I started my software development journey in March of 2022, learning to code online. After creating small javascript projects, I realised that I would love to make this my career. I enrolled in the software development program at the Davis Applied Technical College in Kaysville, Utah in April and completed the course by December.

As coders I think we all find creating projects hands on very satisfying, this is what immediately hooked me. The process of problem solving and the frustration that comes with working through errors is what makes coding so rewarding. There isn't much better than making your program run, after getting stuck and thinking it was outside of your capabilities. Learning is very valuable and fun to me, and I see software development as an endless supply.
